### Gödel — Standalone Thinker Page
Same format as Kropotkin/Gramsci/Ostrom/Graeber/Hardt-Negri/D&G thinker profiles.
*Every System Has an Outside*
Incompleteness: every formal system contains truths it cannot prove from within itself.
Political translation: every closed power structure has a legitimacy crisis it cannot
resolve on its own terms. The outside that hegemony cannot fully incorporate has a
mathematical proof. Connects Gramsci → Hardt & Negri → Wynter in a new register.
Note: math content may call for slightly different structure — confirm before writing.
