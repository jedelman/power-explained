# EP-03 — The Hardest Thing

*For: Epilogue / Doves*

You've been careful in everything you've written —
the burnout is described somatically but not catastrophically.

Is there something harder underneath?

You don't have to answer this. But the reader who is in the
hardest place needs to know whether the person who wrote this book
has been in the hardest place too.

The question is not: have you suffered enough to speak?
The question is: is there a place in your descent that the book
hasn't yet touched, that the reader in the dark needs to find here?
