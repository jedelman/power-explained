# GT-01 — After Closing

*For: Goetia of Capital (follow-up)*

You described explaining production of subjectivity after the restaurant closed —
with the confidence and clarity of having studied it for years.
"Because, I realized, I had."

Who were you explaining it to?
What was the occasion?

I want the scene of the arrival — not the five years of study
but the moment you heard yourself speaking with fluency
and realized you had already become the person who knew this.
