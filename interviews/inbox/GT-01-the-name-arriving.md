There are a few coffee shops we frequent because they double as salons. If the right group of people at the right time are there and in the right mood, you'll get a cross-pollinating caffeinated frenzy of leftist (sometimes) theory or just the usual bar bullshit without the inebriation. I'm known in these circles as someone who reads theory, so I have a few friends that will check what I'm reading and follow up, or propose topics of their own. We'll riff when it's slow - they're often behind the bar - and keep conversations going over the course of months or years. It's been a really nourishing intellectual practice, if frustrating.
