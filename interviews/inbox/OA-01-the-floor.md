# OA-01 — The Floor

*For: Orgone Accumulator chapter (Reich / the body)*

You said: "I just got slower and slower, and stumbled more,
until one day I stumbled and I couldn't get back up so I just lay there.
When I burned out of software, I lay down for 4 months."

What did those 4 months actually look like?

Not the narrative — the texture. What did you do on a Tuesday at 2pm?
Did you leave the house? What did you eat? What was the quality of light
in whatever room you were in? Did you sleep at normal hours?

The reader who is currently on the floor needs this.
They need to know what the floor actually looks like from the inside —
not as failure, but as a specific place with its own geography.
