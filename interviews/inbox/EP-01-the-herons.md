# EP-01 — The Actual Words

*For: Epilogue / Doves*

You said: "we tell the herons, with our actual words,
that we love them and wish them good luck."

What do you actually say?

Verbatim if you can. Or as close as you can get.

This is not a small ask — it's the most intimate practice in the book,
and the reader needs to hear the actual words, not a description of them.
The specific words are the practice. The summary is not.
