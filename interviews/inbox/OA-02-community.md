# OA-02 — What Community Did

*For: Orgone Accumulator chapter (Reich / the body)*

The only reason you got up after 4 months was to go be in the woods, in community.

What did community do that the floor couldn't?

Not philosophically — specifically. What happened in the first week
in the woods that your body registered as different?
Was it a meal? A specific person? Something someone said or didn't say?
The quality of silence around a fire?

The distinction between being alone with your thoughts and being
held by a group of people in a physical place — I want to know
what that felt like from the inside, on day 3 or day 5, before
you had language for it.
