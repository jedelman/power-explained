# WW-03 — Your Quest

*For: Wizard's Walk chapter*

>The Wizard's Walk chapter ends by giving the reader a specific quest:
one thing, in one place, that only they can do.

>What's yours?

>Not the book — the book is the map, not the territory.
Something more local, more bodily, more specific.

>What is Jason building or protecting or tending right now
that only Jason can do? What is the thing that will not happen
if you don't show up for it?

I have a vision for cyberpunk that I've come to realize only I hold. A sacred relationship between man and machine that puts us in right relation with the Earth and each other. In my writing and my music and my being, I carry the vision as I walk, sharing as much as I can.
