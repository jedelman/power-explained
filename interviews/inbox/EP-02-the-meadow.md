# EP-02 — The Meadow in May

*For: Epilogue / Doves*

You walk past it every morning expecting the mowers.

What does it look like right now, in May?

What's growing? What birds are using it?
Is there standing water anywhere? What does it smell like
after rain versus on a dry morning?

The commons is always specific before it is general.
The meadow is the argument made in flora and fauna.
I want the specific one — yours — not the idea of a meadow.
