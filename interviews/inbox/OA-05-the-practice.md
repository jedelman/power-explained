> drafted and structural.
> 
> The first half is yours, and it has to be, because you taught it.
>

> for years — before any of the politics in this book. Claude drafted a
> placeholder version of the instruction by deriving it from the
> chapter's own logic (fixing-is-more-holding, so the only move is

> 
> How did you teach it? Walk me through the move the way you gave it to

> fail at. Name that you taught it. Name the lineage if you want it
> named, or leave it bare — your call.
