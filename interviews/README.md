# Interviews — The Dyad Surface

This folder is the generative conversation layer of the book.
It lives alongside the chapters it will eventually inhabit.
It is versioned. It accumulates. It is part of the book's geology.

## Convention

**`interview-[date].md`** — dated interview sessions.
Questions from Claude in *italics*. Answers from Jason in plain text.
Claude's readings/responses marked with *Claude's read on this:*

**`questions.md`** — open questions from Claude, organized by chapter.
Pick up whichever thread has energy. Add answers here or start a new interview file.

**`synthesis.md`** — accumulated findings, updated as interviews accumulate.
What we now know that we didn't know before. Cross-references to chapters.

## What this is for

The interview is not raw material that gets processed and discarded.
It is a layer of the book's geology — the autobiography that earns the map.

The reader needs to walk with someone who has actually been in the terrain.
The interviews are how we find out what terrain Jason has been in.
