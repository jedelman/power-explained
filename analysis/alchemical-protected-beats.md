# Alchemical pass — protected beats inventory

**What this file is.** The protected-material inventory for the alchemical
prose-refinement pass (started 2026-06-10). The pass dissolves what weakens and
recomposes what reveals — but the book's woven gold must survive the solvent.
This file lists, chapter by chapter in river order, the material the pass must
**not cut or de-name**: the wizard-lexicon school/technique namings and woven
beats (quoted verbatim from the gesture bodies), the recurring motif sites, the
gestures that belong to published threads (paths), and the beauty_check
gestures (deletion-only under the 2026-06-10 re-gating — itemize any deletion
in the commit header).

**How to use it.** Editors: read your chapter's section before touching the
chapter. Critics: receive the chapter's section with the critique prompt; a
draft that drops or weakens a protected beat fails. A protected sentence may be
moved (within its gesture or chapter) if the naming survives intact and the
weave it serves still lands; it may not be cut, paraphrased into ordinary
prose, or stripped of its school name. Thread members carry path obligations —
their text is rendered in `/book/path/<slug>` as well as the chapter; cutting
or heavily rewriting one breaks the thread. Motif gestures carry the book's
long rhymes (herons, Atlantic City, the cook, the shrug, singularity, the
cedar chip, the buried-because-threatening chiasm); keep the motif language
when editing them.

**Provenance.** Generated 2026-06-10 by case-insensitive extraction over the
gesture bodies of every chapter in `src/content/manifests/the-river.manifest.yml`
(28 chapters), terms per the wizard lexicon (`for-agents/wizard-lexicon.md`)
and the composition ledger. Every quoted sentence is verbatim from its gesture
body; every ID was verified against its chapter manifest. Incidental
(non-lexicon) uses of common words were filtered by judgment and are noted
inline where relevant.
