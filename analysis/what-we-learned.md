# What we learned — the alchemical and image-weave passes

*Written into the institution so it isn't lost (the book's own argument).
Distilled from ~40 blind reader-witnesses across two full passes, 2026-06-10
to 2026-06-13. Jason + Claude, the centaur.*

---

## About the book

1. **It works where it DOES what it talks about; it falters where it TALKS
   ABOUT what it's doing.** Every gesture the alchemical pass retired was an
   announcement. Every chill the image-weave dissolved was a lapse into
   telling. The whole two-pass arc was one correction in a hundred costumes:
   get the book to stop narrating itself and just be itself.

2. **The two passes were *solve et coagula* at project scale.** The
   alchemical pass SUBTRACTED (−0.9% words, essence over arithmetic) — the
   book was too full of announcement. The image-weave ADDED (new prose,
   locks opened) — the book was too thin of inhabited image in its theory
   sections. Opposite deficits, opposite remedies, one goal.

3. **Theory chills as summary / citation / announcement; it warms through
   persons, images, and felt-first delivery.** Confirmed across the whole
   corpus. The nature-image interview was the delivery vehicle, and it
   worked: chilled theory sections became the chapters' strongest beats
   ("the reparations argument has never landed for me through any other
   framing").

## About reading (what the witnesses taught us)

4. **The body leads.** "Somatic before semantic" recurred in nearly every
   report — "landed in the chest before I understood why," "I noticed my
   jaw," "nausea, not metaphorically." Independent, repeated empirical
   evidence for the book's own thesis about the body, gathered from readers
   who knew nothing about the book's production.

5. **The restraint is the ask, and the hand-off lands.** "Mine to do,"
   "invited not assigned," "the chapter trusted me," "the author named
   theirs first." The Stone criterion — the transformation continuing
   without the alchemist — was met chapter by chapter and at book scale.

6. **Some chills are inherent and shouldn't be chased.** Familiarity (a
   theory the reader has already met) and genuine conceptual density (the
   16/16b AI material) resist any image. Distinguish a falter you fix from a
   roughness that's load-bearing. Log the difference; don't dress it up. Two
   weaves were honest partials (the OA grapevine, neutral; the ch-16 lawn,
   architecturally complete but the familiarity slide persisted).

## About the method

7. **The master image must come from the author and should already be
   latent in the work.** Tracking — consciousness as pattern recognition
   conditioned by the substrate conditioned by history — was not imported.
   It was the book's own practice (the deer, the stalker's gait, the
   zanjera) promoted to the explanatory frame. A good metaphor decorates;
   this one made the book conscious of its own method.

8. **The image must BE the explanation — inhabited and extended, never an
   attached simile.** This is the line the centaur's AI half keeps trying to
   cross. The critic caught it repeatedly.

9. **Consistency IS the incantation — extended from words to images.** The
   sneaked name (one motion-word per school) became the image-map (one
   reserved family per concept). Collisions are reverts. The discipline
   guards the book in both directions — it caught the heron-as-spoor
   collision and the musical-metaphor smuggled into a nature image before
   either shipped.

10. **Provenance is not bookkeeping — it is the refusal the book argues
    for.** Tracing every image to the author's lived experience kept the
    centaur from doing the exact thing the book condemns: separating
    knowledge from its bearer. The provenance rule is the book's ethics
    applied to its own making.

## About the collaboration (the centaur, learned from inside)

11. **The division is real and asymmetric.** The author brings the lived
    image with provenance at the source, the judgment that something
    corresponds to experience, and the master frame. The AI brings the speed
    of synthesis, the tireless loop, holding the whole architecture at once.
    Neither could produce what the two made.

12. **The AI's failure mode is elegance.** "More elegant than honest" (the
    Stone witness, on the centaur section); the attached similes; the
    smuggled musical metaphor; the invented four-word transition caught
    before commit. The averaging machine reaches for the smooth, the
    plausible, the well-formed — and is most dangerous exactly where it is
    most confident. The loop exists because that taste cannot be trusted at
    its own peak.

13. **The loop is a perceptual commons.** A fresh-context Opus critic plus a
    fresh blind witness who knows nothing = a reader who cannot be told what
    to feel. No change was trusted on taste alone. This is what makes the
    collaboration trustworthy — and it is the book's own thesis
    (consciousness as commons) applied to its own body.

## The recursion

The book argues that consciousness is a commons, that attention is the
enclosure beneath all the others, that the singular is precisely what
averaging destroys. We refined it by running a perceptual commons, tracking
the substrate of each chapter (where it chills, where it warms), and
refusing to let the averaging machine flatten the lived image. The method
enacted the thesis. The wound — a book against the enclosure of language,
improved using the enclosure engine — stayed the most honest feature,
because the loop kept it visible.
