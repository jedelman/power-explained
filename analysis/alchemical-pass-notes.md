standard, the frozen prompts, and the retirement checklist live in
`for-agents/alchemical-pass-instructions.md` — read that first. Protected
material per chapter: `analysis/alchemical-protected-beats.md`.

The frame: solve et coagula. Remove what weakens or corrupts; restructure to
reveal essence. Every chapter is tested twice — by the craft critic against
its essence statement, and blind by a reader-witness against its invocation.
The Stone test at wrap-up: a blind witness walks the whole refined river and
reports the arc against the title's promise.

### prologue (P-PR) — 3 EDITS, ALL PASS · witness MATCH · 2026-06-10

- **Essence:** The wound and the gift on facing pages: the rent, the bill,
  and the highway are one structure with a name — enclosure — and the
  commons is what people keep building in its cracks. Central image: the
  night herons — look up. The turn: we have something the herons don't; we
  can name it.
- **Invocation:** The reader's scattered private frustrations fuse into one
  nameable pattern; they receive the book's address as a description of
  themselves and the heron's red eye as proof they still belong to the
  world. They leave holding the word *enclosure* and the promise that seeing
  is the preparation. (Second-initiation station: look up.)
- **Edits (3):**
  - RETIRED G-PR-020 ("This is an unusual book in a different, related
    way.") — scaffolding announcement; the centaur paragraph carries the tie
    explicitly. Ruling 1 applied (section break preserved). Critic PASS;
    found the cut reveals an anaphora across the break ("This book is…about
    those people." / "This book has two authors.") that quietly implies the
    authors are among those people.
  - G-PR-013: "In order to create" → "To create" — connective flab; the
    protected care-escalation untouched. Critic PASS.
  - G-PR-009: "as much as us" → "as much as we do" — revised after two
    independent flags (editor + blind witness stumble); single-hunk
    re-critique PASS.
- **Witness (blind):** MATCH. "The rent, the hospital bill, the highway,
  and the app are the same thing — not metaphorically, but as one process";
  enclosure received as "a present-tense verb, something happening to me
  continuously"; the Stone criterion again unprompted: "The doing is kept as
  mine, deliberately… I felt trusted rather than instructed, and that made
  me more willing, not less."
- **Witness observations:** (1) FLAG FOR JASON — the six-levels section

  be warmed back up." The map-of-the-book is an authorial structure; not
  touched. Paired with the craft critic's independent observation that the
  closing via-negativa triple ("not a manifesto… not a policy book… not an
  academic book… What it is: a guide") edges the definition anti-pattern.
  Both go to Jason as one question: does the prologue's orientation
  machinery (the map + the positioning) earn its temperature drop, or is
  there an alchemical recomposition of it? (2) The "earth flowers" close of
  the six-levels section pulled the witness back hard — whatever happens,
  that landing stays.
