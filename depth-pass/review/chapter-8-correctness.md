| Para. 2 (DeGraffenreid intro) | "The company had not hired any Black women before 1964" | "had not hired any Black women for assembly line work before 1970" | Court record establishes GM did not hire Black women for assembly line production until 1970. Before that, only one Black woman held any position (as a janitor). The 1964 date conflates the Civil Rights Act passage year with the actual hiring history. Sources: DeGraffenreid v.
