- **Night heron physical description** ("red eye," "white plumes," "heavy-bodied, short-legged"): Accurate for yellow-crowned night herons — dark orange-to-red eyes, white crown plumes in breeding season, stocky body, short legs.
- **English enclosure "roughly the fifteenth through the nineteenth centuries"**: Accurate. Enclosure received comment from the fifteenth century (John Rous petitioned Parliament) and the process continued through the nineteenth century.
- **Mondragon / "worker-owned cooperatives in the Basque country"**: Accurate. Mondragon Corporation, founded 1956, is in the Basque region of Spain.

- **Zuccotti Park in 2011**: Accurate. Occupy Wall Street began September 17, 2011 in Zuccotti Park.
- **Paulo Freire's sugarcane worker**: Accurate. Freire implemented literacy education with sugarcane workers in Brazil; the specific image of writing one's name as a moment of consciousness is consistent with his documented work.

"Waist-deep in a rising river" is a specific image; if this is presented as a literal account rather than metaphor, verify against Roy's documented activism.
