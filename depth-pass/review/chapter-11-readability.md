# Readability Pass: The Circle in the Blizzard (Chapter 11)

## Changes Made

| Location | Original | Revised | Reason |
|----------|----------|---------|--------|
| Kropotkin section, Siberian horses passage (line 29) | "when the temperature drops past surviving alone, they form a tight circle" | "when the temperature drops past what they can survive alone, they form a tight circle" | "drops past surviving alone" is grammatically awkward — "past" needs a noun-phrase object, and "surviving alone" (a gerund) doesn't work naturally as a spatial or threshold reference. A reader pauses. "Past what they can survive alone" resolves the ambiguity without altering meaning or rhythm. |
| Graeber death sentence (line 49) | "writing and organizing and practicing, until the end, what he had spent thirty years arguing for" | "writing and organizing and practicing until the end what he had spent thirty years arguing for" | The commas around "until the end" create an unnatural pause that breaks the participial chain before it reaches its object. Without the commas, the three participles (writing, organizing, practicing) flow through "until the end" and land cleanly on "what he had spent thirty years arguing for." With the commas, a reader expects the sentence to close or shift after "practicing." |

## Notes

The chapter is in strong shape. The voice is consistent throughout: direct, grounded, unhurried, intellectually precise without being instructional. Only two genuine stumbles found, both minor.

The chapter's formal risk — moving through thinkers by what they demonstrate rather than chronologically, weaving personal testimony into theoretical passages without signaling transitions — pays off. The execution is controlled.

The Graeber section on the three primordial economic forms ("communism," "exchange," "hierarchy") uses "communism" as a technical term distinct from Soviet communism. This is a conceptual challenge, not a prose problem, and outside the readability pass scope.

Word count is already lean. No significant cutting is possible through prose tightening alone without losing substance; the density is proportionate throughout.
