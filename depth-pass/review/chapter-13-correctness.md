The North End does have an Irish history (the Irish were dominant 1840s–1880s) but this is not what Jacobs documented. |

- **Mesopotamian clay tablets:** The description of credit records from ancient Mesopotamia as among the first financial instruments is consistent with the historical record Graeber draws on.
- **100 million adults with medical debt in 2023:** Confirmed by KFF Health News/NPR investigation and multiple sources.
- **JAMA Network Open 2024 study / 1 percentage point → extra year of life lost per 1,000 people:** Confirmed. The study found 1.12 years of life lost per 1,000 people per one-percentage-point increase in medical debt prevalence; "extra year" is an accurate rounding.
- **People in problem debt three times more likely to consider suicide:** Confirmed by Money and Mental Health Policy Institute research and multiple UK/US studies; adjusted odds ratios of approximately 3.39.
- **India farmer suicides 1995–2018 ~400,000 / ~48 per day:** Confirmed by NCRB data as reported in Scroll.in and academic research.

- **US farmer suicide rate 3.5x general population:** Confirmed across National Rural Health Association, AgWeb, Drovers, and academic sources.
- **US farm debt at all-time high of $416 billion in 2019:** Confirmed by USDA ERS data and multiple press reports.

- **Latonya Suggs — $63,000 in debt from two-year criminal justice program at Everest:** Confirmed in multiple press accounts of the Corinthian 15 debt strike.
- **Makenzie Vasquez — $30,000 from a medical assistant certificate:** Confirmed. CBS San Francisco and other sources report she was "more than $30,000 in debt" from her program at Everest College in San Jose; she dropped out after six months because she couldn't afford the monthly fees.

- **Credit unions holding over $2 trillion in US assets:** Confirmed. NCUA data shows federally insured credit unions holding $2.43 trillion as of December 2025; "over $2 trillion" is accurate.
- **Mondragon / Caja Laboral:** The description of Caja Laboral's origin and function is consistent with established sources. Caja Laboral was founded in 1959 as part of the Mondragon cooperative network.
- **Rolling Jubilee "two cents on the dollar":** Consistent with publicly reported purchase ratios for distressed medical debt portfolios.
- **Bourdieu on the uncertainty of return distinguishing reciprocity from transaction:** Accurate attribution. Bourdieu's writing on the time-lapse between gift and counter-gift as the mechanism that distinguishes gift exchange from commodity exchange is well-documented.
