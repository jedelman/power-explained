## Notes

This is well-controlled, rhythmically intentional prose. The only genuine stumble in the full chapter was the elided "needed kept" construction in paragraph 16. Every other sentence that might invite editorial attention turned out on examination to be doing deliberate rhetorical work — anaphora, compressed accusation, strategic repetition, managed syntactic weight.

The flagged correctness issues (Robinson's biographical background, Berkeley/FSM timing) will require attention before publication, but they are factual questions, not prose problems. Once resolved, no additional readability work is needed in those passages.

Word count is already disciplined. No bloat identified; no trimming recommended.
