## Notes

The confirmed correction around *Empire* and Negri's imprisonment is the most significant factual error in the chapter. The original sentence conflated Negri's *early* prison experience (1979–1983, during which *The Savage Anomaly* was written) with his later return to prison in 1997, and attributed *Empire* to both periods — when in fact *Empire* was written in exile in France. The corrected version preserves the rhetorical connection to Gramsci while accurately placing the prison experience in Negri's biography.

The "subtitle added by editor" claim about "Lifeboat Ethics" is a specific anecdote that may be true but needs a primary source or editorial record. The article's subtitle is part of its standard bibliographic record; Hardin's personal objection to it is the unverified element.

The 1965 vs. 1973 Workshop founding is a minor timeline issue that the author may wish to clarify with a phrase like "eventually founding" or "would go on to found."
