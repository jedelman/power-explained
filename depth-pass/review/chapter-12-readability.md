## Notes

This is a strong chapter with well-controlled voice and pacing. The prose moves at different tempos across sections — the Narmada opening is compressed and declarative; the Freire section builds clause-work; the personal anecdote opens up into longer sentences before tightening at the close. That variation is working.

One change was made: the repeated phrase in paras. 93 and 95. Everything else is intentional or borderline. The chapter does not have the explanatory scaffolding problem that appeared in earlier pieces — it trusts the reader throughout.

The inline FLAG and CORRECTION markers added during the correctness pass interrupt readability of the raw file, but they are working documents for human review, not permanent prose elements.

The most significant issue for the author's attention remains the Freire timeline flag from the correctness pass: "in the same decade Roy was learning to report, Paulo Freire was running adult literacy programs" implies contemporaneity that is not historically accurate. If the parallel is structural rather than chronological, a small adjustment — "In an earlier decade" or simply dropping "in the same decade" — resolves it without altering the argument.
