## Notes

This is a high-quality chapter. The prose is intentionally rhythmic — short declarative sentences alternating with long accumulating ones — and the rhetorical structures (anaphora, deliberate fragments, asyndeton) are doing real work. The two comma fixes are the only changes that meet the standard of "a real reader would stumble here." Everything else is either intentional style or acceptable on the first read.

The long final paragraph (from "There is a tension I want to name...") is structurally unusual — a single dense block where the rest of the chapter breathes with white space. This may be intentional (the author making a different kind of move at the end — reflective, not rhetorical). Worth flagging as something Jason might want to reconsider in revision, but it is not a readability problem per se: the sentences within it are clear.

The chapter does not need cutting. It earns its length. The historical sections move quickly; the personal section is doing something different and requires its density to land.
