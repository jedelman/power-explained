invocation: >-
  The outside is not a place on the map. It is a relation of power — the
  margin made by excluding, terrain the machine can draw value from without
  full payment. Look at any system; find where the math doesn't close. There
  you will find one. It is not the excess of the system. It is the mechanism,
  maintained — and when the geography runs out, the machine marks new ones:
  debt, attention, the care that was never paid for. The outside is wherever
  the accounting stops. Not everything beyond the accounting is the outside;
  some things cannot be enclosed at all. So enter the territory you were
  trained to read as empty.
