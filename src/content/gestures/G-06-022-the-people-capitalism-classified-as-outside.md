The people capitalism classified as outside the human were not only victims of racial capitalism. From their position, its racial structure was visible in ways it was not visible from inside. You cannot miss the racial architecture of capitalism if you are property within it. The outside produces suffering. It also produces understanding — a clarity about the system's actual logic that the people inside the system are organized not to see.
