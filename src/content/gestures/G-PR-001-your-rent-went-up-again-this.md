Rent went up again this year. Not a little — enough that we did the math and it doesn't work anymore, not with everything else going up at the same time. You called the landlord. They said there's nothing they can do: costs are up everywhere, they have to stay competitive. You know this isn't quite true. You also know there's nothing you can do about it.
