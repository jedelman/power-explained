Ten years in, by May 2024: six operating worker cooperatives — Freedom Farms (organic vegetables on five acres), Green Team Landscaping, the Community Production Cooperative (3D printing and small-scale fabrication), Zero Waste, Eversville Design and Print Shop, and the Catering Cooperative — with five more in development.

The Fannie Lou Hamer Community Land Trust holds over forty-five properties in West Jackson: five housing units, a community center, two commercial units, a shopping plaza, twelve farming plots. Production linked to cooperative finance, to cooperative education, to community land — held together by people's assemblies and years of political organizing.
