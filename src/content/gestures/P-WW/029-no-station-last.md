There is no station last on the walk. You do not arrive and put the staff down. The wizard returns from the descent more capable than they went in, but they do not come back to a station marked HOME with the porch light on. They come back to the next descent, in a slightly more useful body, with a slightly more practiced eye. There is never enough time. You stop because you ran out of time, not because the work is finished. So when this chapter ends, in a paragraph or two, the walking does not end. The book ends. The walking is what comes after the book.
