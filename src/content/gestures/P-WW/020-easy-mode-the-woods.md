Some psychic architecture is easy to build. When I was running the wilderness program, the architecture was already mostly built. The forest does most of the work. The frame — *we are here to be in this place, and to be paid attention to it by people who have been here longer than us* — is something the guide carries by being there. The container is already standing when you arrive. The wonder fills it on its own. You do not have to convince anyone of the aura; the trees are doing that. You only have to not get in the way. That was easy mode.
