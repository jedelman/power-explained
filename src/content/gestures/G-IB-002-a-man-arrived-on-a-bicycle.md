A man was already there, setting up. Not a cargo bike, not a van — a custom rig, everything self-contained: the speakers mounted low, the battery tucked in, the mixer fixed to the handlebars. No truck. No generator. No permit required, probably. He set up in the street outside the market in the time it takes to unpack a bag.
