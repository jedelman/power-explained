*Hegemony.* Gramsci's name. The script. The demon that writes the other demons' lines and calls it the natural order.

Hegemony is not domination by force. Force is what power uses when hegemony fails. Hegemony is the production of consent — the arrangement of ideas, institutions, and cultural forms such that the dominant order seems natural, obvious, inevitable. The subordinated person is not forced to accept the frame. They are produced inside it, shaped by it from infancy, such that accepting the frame feels like seeing reality clearly.

The specific sensation is a kind of ease — the frame moving with you, the words arriving before you choose them. You do not freeze in hegemony. You flow. The haze where the objection would be. The thought that does not quite form. The amnesia for what you were about to say.
