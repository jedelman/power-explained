Audre Lorde was in dialogue with Combahee through the late 1970s. Black, lesbian, mother, poet, cancer-survivor. She put it sharper than anyone.

*The master's tools will never dismantle the master's house* (1979). *There is no such thing as a single-issue struggle, because we do not live single-issue lives* (1982).

Build different tools. Live the lives we actually live. The frameworks follow.
