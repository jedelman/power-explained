The things we have done with the mind through information technology and through psychedelics are eldritch in this older sense. Not unnatural — a new nature. We have extended perception into frequencies no organism has ever had access to: the electromagnetic spectrum visualized, the microbiome characterized, the neural correlates of experience mapped. We have also discovered that consciousness, subjected to certain compounds, opens onto something that consistently presents as larger than itself — not a fantasy but a contact, an encounter with a field that the ordinary mind is too busy managing its own coherence to notice.
