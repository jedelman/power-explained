Four readers. Different positions on the same field. Same demon.

Robinson reads the interior of capitalism — how race lives in the mechanism. Rodney reads the exterior — how the outside was engineered into underdevelopment. Fanon reads the body and the mind — how the colony enters the self. Mbembe reads the postcolony — how the logic persists when the flag comes down.

One structure under four readings. The chapter calls it *the colony*.
