The Flowering Earth.

Not the sustainable earth — sustainability is a managerial concept, the earth as resource to be kept from depletion. Not the just earth — justice is corrective, the earth as a system to be rebalanced. The Flowering Earth: the earth as a living being in the process of producing beauty, complexity, singularity. Flowering.
