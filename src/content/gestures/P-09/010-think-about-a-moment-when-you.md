Think about a moment when you encountered an idea — maybe about how housing could work, or how a workplace could be organized differently — and your immediate response was *that's not realistic*, before you'd actually thought about whether it was. Not an argument. A reflex. The kind of dismissal that doesn't feel like a decision because it doesn't feel like anything at all. It just feels like knowing.
