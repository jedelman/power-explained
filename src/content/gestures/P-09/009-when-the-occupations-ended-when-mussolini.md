When the occupations ended, when Mussolini rose, when the left that had seemed so powerful collapsed into its opposite — the same workers who had occupied factories now joining Blackshirt rallies — Gramsci was left with a question that refused release. The material conditions for change had been there. The organizational capacity had been there. The moment had been there. Why had the terrain not held?
