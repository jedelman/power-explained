The sixth is the return: the next sunrise, when you wake with the memories coming back to you, and rise to use them. Six chapters on what to actually build: the credit commons, the land commons, the care commons, the knowledge commons, the initiation practices that produce communities capable of governing all of it, and the governance forms that hold a composition of singularities together without dissolving them into uniformity. This part ends in Zuccotti Park in 2011, where everything the book describes happened briefly and imperfectly and pointed somewhere. And then it ends the way the wizard's road always ends: face to face with the living world. On the ground beside the doves. The herons back in the willow oaks, who know things about this place we are still learning to understand.
