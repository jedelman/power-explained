Capital produces the bodies that capital needs.

The school that produces obedient test-takers is not secretly coordinating with the factory that needs obedient line workers. They evolved in the same ecosystem, under the same pressures, toward the same adaptive solution. The result is a population shaped — in their habits, their reflexes, their understanding of what is possible — for the system that shaped them.

When you try to build something that requires a different kind of person, you discover this quickly.
