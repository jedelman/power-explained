The builders have not stopped. Every land trust, every cooperative, every mutual aid network, every commons institution this book has described — these are positions being won right now, in the territory the dying old has abandoned or never reached. The war of position has been underway for a long time. The council movement of 1919, the cooperative movement of 1956, the Zapatista uprising of 1994, the municipal land trust movement that took root in Burlington in 1984, the mutual aid networks of 2020 — not separate attempts but a single thread, running through different hands, accumulating. Not every position held. The thread has never broken.
