plain: >
  To enclose land is to take its aura away. Each piece of land is a one-of-a-
  kind living thing. Enclosure turns it into a number of acres that can be
  traded. It says this piece is the same as that piece. It says what stood
  here is no different from what will stand there. It says one can stand in
  for the other.

  That is a lie. But the whole business of land trading runs on it. The whole
  work that we call progress runs on it. It turns land that has aura into
  empty space that can be bought and sold.

To enclose land is to extract aura. To reduce a unique living relation to a quantity of exchangeable abstract acres is to declare that this piece is the same as that piece, that what stood here is no different than what will stand there, that *substitution is possible.* The whole machinery of speculation runs on this lie. The whole work of progress runs on the conversion of auratic land into exchangeable abstract space.
