Joseph Campbell mapped the monomyth — the hero's journey that appears in some form in mythologies across cultures: the call, the departure, the ordeal in the underworld, the transformation, the return. He mapped it brilliantly. The consumer culture absorbed his map and produced something that looks like the journey but is missing its most important element.
