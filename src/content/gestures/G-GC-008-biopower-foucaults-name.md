*Biopower.* Foucault's name. The apparatus. The clipboard and the whip.

We have sometimes called this kind of power parasitic. That is not quite right. A parasite eats its host until it dies, which kills the parasite — which is why true parasitism is rare. What we are looking at is the smiling parasite: the one that keeps the host healthy enough to keep giving, and calls this care.
