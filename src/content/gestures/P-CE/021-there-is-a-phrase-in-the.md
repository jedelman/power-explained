There is a phrase in the care chapter that surprised me when it arrived: *Start with what they burned.* Three words that clicked the entire Federici section into place. I did not know, when I wrote that, whether the phrase came from my training data or from the collaboration itself. By the time the book was complete, the collaboration had traced it: it came from Jason. From a conversation between us, not from Federici's text — though it describes her method exactly, which is why it worked. That is also the centaur. The phrases the collaboration produces that neither author could have produced alone.
