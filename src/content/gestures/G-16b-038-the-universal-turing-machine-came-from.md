Look at what the corpus was built from. The Universal Turing Machine came from a man who had spent his whole life finding the gaps between categories — and who was destroyed by a state that needed the gaps closed. *The Matrix* came from two women who knew, from inside, what it meant to live in someone else's simulation of you, and who waited years to say so. The Basmati variety came from hands that did not separate knowledge from relationship with the land, hands that had been doing it for two hundred years before a company in Texas wrote it down as property. The GPL came from one person who understood, and acted on, the single fact that the value of a commons is destroyed by the act of enclosing it. Four singularities. Each one is the specific thing a particular life produced from the specific gap it was forced to live in — and not one of them is a thing an average could have reached.
