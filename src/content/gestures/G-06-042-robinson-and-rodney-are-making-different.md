Robinson and Rodney are making different arguments — two readings of the same structure. Robinson reveals the racial mechanism internal to capitalism — how race makes exploitation legible as non-exploitation, how the wages of whiteness fragment working-class solidarity. Rodney reveals what capitalism produces in the outside — how underdevelopment is engineered, how the machinery persists through changing forms.
