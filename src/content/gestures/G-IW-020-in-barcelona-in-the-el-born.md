In Barcelona, in the El Born neighborhood, I heard music coming from a side street before I could see where it was coming from. Someone had set up a rig — instruments, a small sound system, a gathering of people who had arranged to be there. This wasn't spontaneous. It was community. The distinction matters. Spontaneity is alone. Community is a practice that has been built by many people over time until it feels, from the outside, like it just happened.
