*Biopower.* Foucault's name. The apparatus. The clipboard and the whip.

We have sometimes called this kind of power parasitic. That is not quite right. A parasite eats its host until the host dies, which kills the parasite — which is why true parasitism is rare in nature, and self-destructive when it appears. We obsess over the parasite because we are projecting something we have not yet been able to face about our own situation. What we are looking at is the smiling parasite: the one that keeps the host healthy enough to keep giving, and calls this care.
