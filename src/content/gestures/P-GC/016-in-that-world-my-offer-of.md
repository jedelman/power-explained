In that world, my offer of autonomy was not liberation. It was abandonment. The freedom I was extending was not recognizable as freedom because it had no authority behind it. Nobody had sanctioned it. Nobody had told him this was the correct thing to want. And without that sanction, the wanting couldn't quite activate.
