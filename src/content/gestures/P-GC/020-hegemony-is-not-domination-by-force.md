Hegemony is not domination by force. Force is what power uses when hegemony fails. Hegemony is the production of consent — the arrangement of ideas, institutions, and cultural forms such that the dominant order seems natural, obvious, and inevitable. Not imposed from outside, but generated from within. The subordinated person is not forced to accept the frame. They are produced inside it, shaped by it from infancy, such that accepting the frame feels like seeing reality clearly.
