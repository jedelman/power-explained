This is the thing the enclosure cannot produce. It can produce compliance. It can produce performance. It can produce the appearance of connection, maintained by incentive. But the actual thing — the felt sense of being recognized, of mattering to the people you are with, of existing in their attention — this the enclosure systematically destroys, because it is the condition for everything it cannot control.
