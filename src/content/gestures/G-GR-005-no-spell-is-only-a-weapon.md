No spell is only a weapon. Every one was turned against us, and every one can be turned back — the spell becomes the counter-spell, the averaged self becomes the gift. But the turning is neither symmetric nor safe: a commons can be enclosed again, and a thing freed too fast becomes the next thing for sale. To wield a force you must know which way it points, and be willing to point it home.
