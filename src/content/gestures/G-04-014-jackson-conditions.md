Jackson, Mississippi: median household income around $33,000; poverty rate approaching one in three among Black residents; a state legislature that passed a law in 2023 expanding state-appointed courts with jurisdiction over Jackson specifically — a maneuver so naked that the NAACP called it a modern form of plantation governance. The water system has failed repeatedly. The state has refused to fund repairs at levels even close to what its own engineers say are needed.
