You cannot classify people as available for extraction without a prior framework that places them outside the category of the fully human. You cannot produce colonized subjects who cooperate in their own subjection without a prior classification of what the human is that places the colonized outside it. Necropolitics requires a prior decision that certain lives are not worth managing. The racial architecture of capitalism requires a prior framework that makes the classification legible as something other than crime. All of it rests here: a specific, historical, constructed definition of the human that was designed to exclude and has been functioning as designed ever since.
