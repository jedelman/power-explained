Alongside this: the enclosure of common land, happening in the same centuries, converting shared fields and forests into private property, displacing peasant communities, creating the landless workforce capitalism needed. The two enclosures were part of the same transformation. The enclosure of land made communities dependent on wages. The destruction of communal care knowledge made communities dependent on the market for the things they had produced themselves. The domestic sphere was never outside the economy. It was the economy's unacknowledged foundation.
