The garden the tenant union plants together. The free library in the lobby of the cooperative. The music at the rally that isn't between speeches. These are not the fun parts of organizing that you add when you have time. They are the mechanism by which communities renew themselves — by which people learn that they are capable of more than they were told, by which the excess gets generated that makes movements irreversible in ways that policy victories are not. The renewal is carried in the felt register — felt before it is understood.
