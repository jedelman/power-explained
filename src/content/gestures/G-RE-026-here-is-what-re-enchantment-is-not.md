Here is what re-enchantment is not.

It is not the belief that everything is nice. The animated world contains predation, parasitism, disease, and death. The crow broadcasting the owl's location is alarmed. The alarm is real. The owl is dangerous to the robin's eggs. The re-enchanted world is not a world without violence or loss or the specific cruelty of things eating each other. It is a world in which those things are legible. The crow's alarm is information. The owl's presence is a signal. The pattern of who is alarmed and who is hunting and who is hidden tells you something true about the specific place you are standing.
