Casting has kinds, and learning to read them is the work. Some spells hide a structure; some reveal it; some break it open. Some gather a crowd by need; some cast a people out. Some bind the living to the dead; some summon a future and live as if it were already here. You do not need the whole list. You need to feel which one is in the room.
