I will name mine, because the chapter would not be fair if it asked you to and did not. I have a vision for cyberpunk that I have come to realize only I hold — a sacred relationship between man and machine that puts us in right relation with the Earth and each other. In my writing and my music and my being, I carry the vision as I walk, and I share it as much as I can. That is the sentence. That is the whole thing. It does not require anyone to be impressed by it. It does not require a movement. It requires that I carry it, in the actual rooms I am in, today and tomorrow. That is what makes it the quest. That is what makes it not transferable. If I drop it, it will not be picked up — not because it is precious, but because it is shaped to the specific size and weight of the person carrying it, and I am the only one shaped like this.
