The same spell that lays itself on the mind lays itself on the body. We call that *inscription*. The children had been carrying their bodies the way the world they were born into had taught them — eyes forward, breath shallow, feet placed for pavement. The deer asked the body for a different gait. The body remembered. Inscription too can be undone, given the conditions. The stalking gait was a counter-inscription. The flesh can be taught again.
