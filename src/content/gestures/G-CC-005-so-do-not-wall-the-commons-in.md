So do not wall the commons in for safety; a wall is only the next gate, and the demon is patient. The counter is not protection. It is rupture: break the enclosure open again, and re-common what spills out before the gate can move. The spell that freed the web the first time was just this, written down — take it, use it, share it, never close it. The commons is not a place you reach. It is a rupture you keep making. Make it again.
