The cook is not an informal arrangement. The cook is written into the governance structure of the *zanjera* — into the same documents that specify water allocation, labor obligation, dispute resolution, and the election of officers. The community does not feed each other while rebuilding the dam because someone thought it would be nice. The governance requires it — because the people who built these institutions understood, across the generations, that rebuilding is not separable from the relationship that makes rebuilding possible. That relationship must be fed.
