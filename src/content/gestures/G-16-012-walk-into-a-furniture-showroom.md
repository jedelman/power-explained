Walk into a furniture showroom — or scroll through a design influencer's feed, which is the same place rendered in light. Everything is coherent. Everything references everything else. The couch speaks to the lamp speaks to the art print, and together they produce an atmosphere that means *successful* or *tasteful* or *arrived* without any single element being responsible. It is the coherence of a lawn. A lawn is the anti-forest: one species, mowed to a single height, every blade interchangeable, nothing allowed to individuate, the soil underneath going dead and the water running off because no roots go deep. It looks like nature and it is the opposite of the forest you just walked through — the network replaced by a monoculture. The showroom is a lawn for the self.
