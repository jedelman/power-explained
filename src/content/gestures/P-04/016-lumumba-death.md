Lumumba was sworn in on July 1, 2013. He died on February 25, 2014, at the age of 66. His son, Chokwe Antar Lumumba, an attorney like his father, was in court when his father called complaining of tightness in his chest. He drove him to St. Dominic Hospital. After tests, after waiting, after a transfusion, Lumumba cried out about his heart, shook, and lost consciousness. He had been mayor for less than eight months.

The Hinds County coroner did not perform an autopsy. The family commissioned an independent one. Asked about claims that the mayor had been assassinated, Chokwe Antar Lumumba said: "We know that our father was loved and appreciated by many and a number of people both in Jackson and around the world have inquired into the manner of his death. At this time, there has been no information provided to the family other than that provided at the time of his death by the doctors. The family will explore all possible causes of his death."

The cause of death has never been definitively settled in public. What is settled is what happened next.
