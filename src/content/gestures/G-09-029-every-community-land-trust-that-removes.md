Every community land trust that removes housing from the speculative market permanently — a position won. Not a reform of the market. A piece of terrain reorganized under different assumptions, producing different common sense about what housing is for. Every worker cooperative. Every mutual aid network that outlasts the crisis that created it. These are positions in the war of position, accumulated across decades, building the terrain on which a different politics becomes possible.

These are not waiting for the revolution. They *are* the revolution, happening at the speed that permanent change actually happens.
