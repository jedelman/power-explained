You have walked. Whatever else is true — whatever you skipped, whatever you went back to, whichever doors you took and which you closed without opening — your body has carried this book across some distance it did not know it was crossing. You are not at a finish line. There isn't one. But the walking has happened. There is something in the way your attention sits at this page that was not there at the prologue, even if you cannot name it. So before we go any further: that is the first good thing. The walking is what this chapter is about, and the fact that you have done some of it is what makes the rest of the chapter possible.
