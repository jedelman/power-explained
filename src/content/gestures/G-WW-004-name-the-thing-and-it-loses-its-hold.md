The first capacity is the one the spellbook taught. A thing with a name is a thing you can get your hands on. Production of subjectivity. Biopower. Hegemony. Conscientização. Enclosure. The shrug. The held jaw. The kid the system did not let build a self. The point of the names was never that the names are correct — the point was that you could finally tell, in a room, what was running, and stop mistaking it for the way things have to be. The wizard names. Not to be clever. To make the thing handle-able by the room.
