This is already happening. You have met them in this book: the Zapatista caracoles, still governing two decades on, the uprising they grew out of three. Cooperation Jackson, building from the most marginalized. The Crown Heights mutual aid network, still meeting. The data cooperatives being built before the AI enclosure completes. The communities of practice that meet every week — imperfect, pointed somewhere, refusing to wait for permission.
