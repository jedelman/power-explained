Into this valley, in February of 1941, a twenty-five-year-old priest arrived. He had been sentenced to death by Franco's forces during the war and escaped the firing squad through what is recorded as an administrative oversight. His name was José María Arizmendiarrieta. He would later write: *La transformación del mundo pasa por la transformación del hombre.* The transformation of the world passes through the transformation of the human being.
