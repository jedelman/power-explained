*Come find us.* The *us* is real — the people who have been walking this terrain in their actual towns, in their actual lives, before you opened the book and while you were reading it and after you put it down. We are not hidden. We are not somewhere else. We are in the room you are in, in the next coffee shop over, in the civic league nobody told you about, on the porch of the house that has been here longer than the developers, in the kitchen of the cooperative that does not advertise. *Come find us* is not a metaphor and it is not just a metaphor. It is both, the way real instructions usually are.
