Not a metaphorical shard. A specific one. A line in a sales contract that says more than it was supposed to say. A street name that records what was displaced to put the street there. An itemized hospital receipt. A clause in your lease. A footnote in a textbook. A patent number on a seed packet. A line in the deed to your house that names the people the land was taken from before it became *yours.* The corner of your city where something keeps happening that nobody planned for. The piece of wood, the stone, the seed, the photograph in a drawer.
