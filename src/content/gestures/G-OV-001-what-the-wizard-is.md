  A wizard is someone who has watched the world long enough to know it
  when it comes back around. They can feel a change coming before it
  arrives, the way you can feel a storm before the sky turns dark.

  job. It is not about robes or towers. It is not about knowing things
  other people are not allowed to touch. A wizard keeps what they know
  down low, in their hands, where it can be used.

A wizard is someone who has walked the terrain long enough to know it the moment it rises again. To feel its weather turn before the sky does. And, knowing it, to reach for a way to be useful. That is the whole job description. Not the robe, not the tower, not a knowledge held above the room like a lamp no one may carry. A wizard's knowing stays low, in the hands.
