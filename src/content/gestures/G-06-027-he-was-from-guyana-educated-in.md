In 1972 Walter Rodney published *How Europe Underdeveloped Africa*. The argument begins with a sentence that reframes the entire conversation:

Africa was not underdeveloped. Africa was developed — into poverty. Not accidentally. Deliberately. By mechanisms that can be identified and traced.
