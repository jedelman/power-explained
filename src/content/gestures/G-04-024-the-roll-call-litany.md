Arizmendi on his bike in the Basque mountains. *Auzolan* in February of 1941, dormant under suppression and not yet returned. Bernie Sanders, elected mayor of Burlington by ten votes in 1981 and not letting the proposal die in 1983. Eight farmers in black blouses in Valencia's cathedral plaza, the Water Tribunal in session for a thousand years. Zanjera farmers in Ilocos rebuilding bamboo dams after the typhoon, the cook feeding everyone while the work gets done. The Champlain homeowner who sold below market in 2003 for a neighbor who did not yet exist. Chokwe Lumumba's campaign, and the eight months, and Rukia speaking at the memorial, and the son who picked it up and lost and got back up. The community fridge on the Brooklyn sidewalk, stocked by whoever walked by that morning. The restaurant in Norfolk that ran for five years. The Zapatistas dissolving their own structure in order to keep building. The caracol.
