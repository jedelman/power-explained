All of it is the commons. All of it is what the enclosure is trying to take. But the enclosure cannot finally take the living world, because the living world is what the enclosure needs to survive. It doesn't own the commons. It parasitizes it. Enclosure is a choice made by living beings to be less than they could be — a bad deal taken out of fear, a lie told to make the reduction feel permanent. You cannot reduce life to property. The life was never property. It was always just life, which is what we have in common.
