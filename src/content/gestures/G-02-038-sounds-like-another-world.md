Sounds like another world, doesn't it?

It is not another world. It is an alternative — accessible from this one, by routes that exist, named in this chapter. The Zapatistas have a phrase that ends *many worlds are possible*. They mean it literally. The arrangements we live under are arrangements, not laws of physics.

A wizard's job is to show you how. To name the conditions. To point at the seams. To lend the seeing that lets you recognize a possible world when you walk into one. And to show you how to reach them — the secret ways, the gathered coalitions, the slow building of the institutional ground that makes different fates possible.

Barcelona is real. So are Naples and Norfolk. The difference between them is not destiny. It is what was done with the conditions each city had.
