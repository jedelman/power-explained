Every person has a *spellbook*. Not a printed book — a personal one, accumulated over years: the incantations they use to navigate the world. Each is different. The wizard who has done the work long enough has gathered the small techniques for loosening what binds, and for calling on what helps: when to speak and when to wait, which silences are productive and which are awkward, what acts on a five-year-old's attention and what releases it. Your first incantation is probably already in your mouth — a phrase you say all the time that isn't a catchphrase. Notice it. That's the first page. The spellbook is not secret. It is informal. It is yours, alone, to keep — to keep deliberately, to add to with intention. Anyone who decides to do this will find others doing the same. The networks exist. You follow the thread.
