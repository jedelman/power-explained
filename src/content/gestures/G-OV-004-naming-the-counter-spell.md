Getting it back begins with naming. Name a thing truly and you can see it coming; you can say *that is what this is*, and the room changes. That is the counter-spell. After it comes the slower work — to read the terrain you're in, to build rooms others can stand in, to hold what most bodies flinch from. The old spellbooks were working lists of what acts on you, drawn so you could act back. This book is one too.
