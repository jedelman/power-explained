When European capitalism emerged from feudalism, it built on these hierarchies rather than discarding them. Certain bodies were available for certain kinds of use. Certain labor was compensated and certain labor was expropriated. And the racial classification system didn't justify exploitation after the fact — it made exploitation legible as something other than exploitation. You cannot steal from someone the system has classified as not having the kind of self that can own things. You cannot commit violence against someone whose suffering the law recognizes as something other than harm.

This is what Robinson called *racial capitalism*: not capitalism plus racism, but capitalism as a racial project from its origins. Race is not the stain on the mechanism. Race is in the mechanism.
