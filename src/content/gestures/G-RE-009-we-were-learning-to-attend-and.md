We were learning to attend. Attention, given consistently and with genuine curiosity, opens a channel. Not metaphorically. The channel is real. Behavioral on both ends. Reciprocal. The birds get as close to you as they feel comfortable. And we began to notice, over months of practice, that when we addressed them — kindly, directly, as one might address a neighbor — they became more comfortable. More present. The distance closed.
