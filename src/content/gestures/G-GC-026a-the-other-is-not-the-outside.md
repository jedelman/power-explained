*The Other.* Not the outside — its opposite.

The outside is a relation of power: terrain under hegemony, the margin made by excluding. Withdraw the power and the outside dissolves. The Other was never made by power at all. The Other is simply another singularity — a person, a creature, a forest, not-you — and it holds the possibility of *magic*: the meeting that becomes, briefly, a single thing that was not there before. Not everything beyond the accounting is the outside. Some things cannot be enclosed at all. The Other is the first of them, and it exists whether power acts on it or not.
