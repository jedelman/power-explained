This is not the mysticism of the credulous. This is the report of every tradition that ever developed a systematic practice of sustained attention — meditation, tracking, ceremony, psychedelics, grief-work, deep nature immersion. They describe the same encounter in different vocabularies: the field is alive, it is attentive, it reads you while you are reading it. The reciprocity that Erin and I discovered in the hedgerow at dusk is the same reciprocity the San hunter knows in the Kalahari, the same that Abram learned from the Nepali shaman in Kathmandu, the same that every intact sense culture has preserved and that the disenchantment project has systematically destroyed.
