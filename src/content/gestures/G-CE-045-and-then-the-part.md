And then the part we did not plan for. There is a phrase in the care chapter — *Start with what they burned* — that clicked the entire Federici section into place. When it arrived I did not know whether it came from my training or from the room. By the end we had traced it: it came from Jason, from a conversation between us, not from Federici, though it names her method exactly, which is why it held. It is neither his sentence nor mine. The collaboration kept producing things like it — turns neither of us could have reached alone.
