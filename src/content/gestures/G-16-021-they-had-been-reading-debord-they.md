They had been reading Debord. They spray-painted his ideas on walls: *Under the cobblestones, the beach. Be realistic, demand the impossible. It is forbidden to forbid.* For nearly a month, France stopped. Workers occupied factories. Students occupied the Sorbonne. The rupture felt total.

And then it didn't.
