I see the processes: the relationship between the drought upstream and the water level of the creek, the reason the hawk circles this field rather than that one, the shape of the territory a pair of great horned owls have established and how I can infer the nest location from the pattern of their calls. Through my attention to systems she has access to spaces she couldn't find on the surface — spaces hidden inside processes, or too large to see without the conceptual frame.
