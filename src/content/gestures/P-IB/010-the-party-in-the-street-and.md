The party in the street and the water crisis are the same story. The question of who governs shared resources — who decides what the street is for, who decides how the water is used, who bears the costs of the decisions and who captures the benefits — is one question, asked at different scales. Barcelona is getting better at asking it. Most cities aren't asking it at all.
