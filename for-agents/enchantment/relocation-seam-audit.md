## 1. becoming-wizards → the-cook  (was → Mondragón)
- **G-IW-031** (close of *Becoming Wizards in the Woods*): "The next chapter is what
  the commons looks like when the work has had time to set… **Eighty years' worth, in
  a mountain town in northern Spain.**"
- Points at **nobody-asked-him-to** (Mondragón / Arizmendi, Basque country). But
  becoming-wizards now opens Part II and is followed by **the-cook** (the zanjera /
  cocinero — not Spain).
- **Fix options:** (a) within Part II, order becoming-wizards → **nobody-asked-him-to**
  → the-cook (puts Mondragón right after the handoff, but separates the two
  commons-proof chapters from the "Learning to See" run); or (b) rewrite G-IW-031's
  close to hand into the-cook generally (drop the Spain-specific image); or (c) accept
  and rewrite to point forward to "the chapters that follow." **My lean: (b)** — keep
  the opener framing the whole Part, not one chapter.

## 2. the-circle-in-the-blizzard → angel-faces  (was → this-was-made / Freire)
- **G-11-039** (close of *The Circle in the Blizzard*): "…teaching a sugarcane worker
  to write his name. **The next chapter is** what…" — points at **this-was-made**
  (Freire, "This Was Made"). Now **angel-faces** (the Angel of History) is inserted
  between them (it opens Part V).
- **Fix options:** (a) rewrite G-11-039's forward ref so it doesn't promise the very
  next chapter (angel-faces is a wizard interlude, then this-was-made delivers Freire);
  or (b) soften to "soon" / "the chapter after the turn." **My lean: (a/b)** — small
  rewrite acknowledging the wizard hinge.

## 3. angel-faces → this-was-made  (was → i-became-the-land / the land trust)
- **G-AB-063** (in *The Angel Faces Backward*): "**The land trust in the next chapter**
  draws the line at the deed." Points at **i-became-the-land** (P-14, the land commons).
  Angel-faces now opens Part V and is followed by **this-was-made** (Roy/Freire) — no
  land trust; i-became-the-land is four chapters later.
- **Fix options:** (a) change "the next chapter" → "a later chapter" / name it
  ("the land commons") without the next-chapter promise; or (b) if this beat is mid-
  chapter, generalize the scale-of-action list it sits in. **My lean: (a)** — drop the
  adjacency claim, keep the example.

## 4. the-hollow-book → goetia  (was → the building chapters)
- **G-16b-057** (close of *The Hollow Book*): "**The next chapter is the practice.**"
  Now followed by **Goetia / The Speaking of Names** (the naming method).
- Naming-as-practice is *plausibly* apt (Goetia teaches the method), but "the practice"
  was written to introduce the build chapters. **Fix options:** (a) accept — Goetia is
  the first practice (naming); or (b) retune the close to point at naming
  ("The next chapter is the naming"). **My lean: (a) accept, or (b) one-word retune.**

## Note on the pattern
All four breaks come from inserting a wizard chapter as a Part-opener *between* an
analytical chapter and the chapter its handoff named. The systemic fix is the same
move each time: **make the handoff frame the Part, not the very next chapter** — which
also reinforces the enchantment thesis (the wizard opener frames; the analytical
chapters unearth). Recommend resolving these before/with the Move 3 weave wrap.
