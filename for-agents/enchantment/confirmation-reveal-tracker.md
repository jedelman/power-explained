# Confirmation-reveal tracker (principle C)

**Principle C (Jason, 2026-06-29):** the final reveal — *you are already part of this,
you have something unique to give, and we need you* — must **confirm** what the reader
has already nearly figured out. The strongest reveal shows readers their *own* earned
insight; we guide them there, but the realization feels like theirs.

This tracks the seed-points where the reader's own dawning self-recognition is planted,
so the arc is deliberate rather than accidental. Surfaced during the Move 3 weave (the
drafters mapped each chapter's confirmation-seed). **Finding: the arc is already built
and intact** across the river — it climbs and lands without a gap.

## Why it holds

The reveal at the-djinn is not sprung — it is the *arrival* of a recognition the reader
has been assembling since the overture. G-18-029 ("You may be one of them already") sets
it up one chapter early, in the reader's own voice, with a bare two-sentence landing; the
djinn then confirms it. No seed needed to be added in Move 3; the arc was already woven by
the prior passes and by the structural moves (the wizard openers frame each Part; the
analytical chapters let the reader unearth the method as their own).

**No intervention proposed.** Recorded so the arc is legible and protected.
