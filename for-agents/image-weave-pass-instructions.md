## The master image (Jason, 2026-06-12 — the law of the pass)

**The tracker reading the substrate.** Consciousness is pattern
recognition; the patterns are conditioned by the substrate; the substrate
is conditioned by history. Soil, organism, plant, climate — and tracker.
This is the book's own practice (the deer, the stalker's gait, birding by
ear, the zanjera, Sand Talk's crocodile) promoted to the explanatory frame
— recognition, not importation.

**The two layers and the middle term:**
- **Tracks & trails** = the reading layer (attention, hegemony-as-habit,
  the six levels as the tracker's arousal sequence). Rows 1, 2, 9.
- **The integrated soil system** = the conditioning layer, history written
  in (cultural fermentation/smell; mycorrhizae; extraction that burns the
  seed bank; erosion when trust-water floods; the lawn). Rows 3, 6, 7, 10,
  11.
- **Organisms in relation** = the middle term (domestication/neoteny/zombie
  ants; agroforestry vs. clear-cut; the grapevine). Rows 4, 5, 8.

**The keystone:** pavement/the lawn. Enclosure paves the substrate so
nothing can be tracked but capital ("hard to track on pavement unless
you're tracking trucks"; "the owners don't want to see any tracks on it").
It destroys legibility itself — looping onto ch-12's "you don't need to
suppress the conclusion if you can enclose the attention that would produce
it." The wetland registers the tiniest creature in fine silt — and the
truck, correctly, as catastrophe.

**The substrate gradient (the desiccated leaf, Jason 2026-06-12):**
living soil composts the fall (commons) → captured soil composts your
participation into someone else's ground (capture) → pavement desiccates,
nothing returns to anyone (enclosure's end state). Three terms; hold them
distinct.

   choreography inviolable. Gesture notes gain the provenance line

   any attached simile; the image must do the explaining; (12) the

   revert + Jason flag.
