# B1 — the You→We seam (proposal, for Jason's approval)

Tier 2. The book's emotional thesis: the lonely "you" is the wound; the audible
"we" is the healing. This proposes a *threshold beat* that stages the turn. The
You/We direct-address voice is Jason's lane — these sketches are starting points
to own or discard, NOT ship-ready. Nothing woven into the river.

## The audit (pronoun arc, river order)

- **The You-isolation zones** (the wound — the system strands you the moment you
  act): G-01-009 ("You can feel the argument for this road…"); G-02-007 ("You
  produced the armor. You handed it to them."); **G-02-023** ("You signed the
  petition because you cared… The structure you were engaging with was designed
  to absorb that energy, not multiply it into change."); G-03-008; **G-03-046**
  (the Ch 3 close: "It's a piece of the world you built").
- **The audible-We arrival**: **G-04-023** (the restaurant — "You have the name
  for this now. What *we* were trying to build was a commons… We offered a $15
  tipped minimum… We closed."). First audible collective reckoning — but it is
  Jason's *biographical* we (wife + friend + the restaurant), not the reader's.
- **Already performs a turn (don't duplicate):** G-04-023 itself. It pivots
  "you have the name" → "what we were trying to build." The turn is real but
  *buried in narrative*, and the "we" is Jason's, not reader-inclusive.

## The finding

The reader's own You→We is never staged. They are left in the "you" at G-03-046;
the first "we" they meet is the Basques' (*they*, G-04-001 Auzolan) and then
Jason's (*his* restaurant, G-04-023). The wound — "the system needs you to think
you act alone" — is never explicitly healed by inviting the reader into a "we."
That healing is the threshold this beat would stage.

## The seam

**End of Chapter 3**, on G-03-046: *"The commons isn't only where you go to get
water. It's what you already are. What enclosure takes isn't just a resource.
It's a piece of the world you built."* — the strongest, loneliest "you" in the
book, immediately before the river turns toward building (the wizards-in-the-
woods interlude, then Ch 4's Auzolan, then the restaurant "we").

Placement options (Jason's call): (a) append the threshold to G-03-046's close;
(b) a new short gesture seated between G-03-046 and the interlude; (c) fold it
into the opening of G-04-023 where the turn already half-happens.

## Two sketches (Jason's voice — rewrite freely)

**Sketch A — spare/concrete (matches the contract's register):**
> It's a piece of the world you built. You built it — and never alone. You only
> thought you were alone because the system needed you to: it strands you the
> moment you act, absorbs what you do, and hands the bill back with your name on
> it. A commons is the proof you were never one of one. We.

Provenance: "a piece of the world you built" (G-03-046); "strands you the moment
you act / absorbs what you do" (voice contract + G-02-023 "designed to absorb
that energy"); "the bill" (overture G-OV-005, "a bill you refuse to pay"); the
landing "We." = the audible-we arriving alone on the page.

**Sketch B — wizard-chiastic (weight/landing):**
> It's a piece of the world you built. The loneliness was the spell. The "we"
> was always the fact — you were only ever made to say "I."

Provenance: the spell/counter-spell frame (the isolation is cast — G-02-023
"designed to absorb"); the contract (the You is the engineered wound); chiasm.
