# Wizard Lexicon — the book's core concepts in wizard register

The consistent vocabulary for *How to Become a Wizard and Save the World*.
Every chapter holds the same register for the same idea — this is voice
consistency at the level of concept. Built collaboratively with Jason during
the sequencing pass (2026-05-26, restructured 2026-06-04).

## The engine

- **The field:** enclosure ⇄ commons. The one axis everything moves along.
- **The irreducible:** magic — composition of singularities acting on each other.
  The averaging machine cannot capture it; it is what perforates the enclosure.
- **The apparatus:** the grimoire (the tool), the wizard (who wields it),
  initiation (the becoming).

## The causal structure — two axes, one rhizome

Idealism and materialism do not compete here. They are two linked causal axes,
and change moves *rhizomatically* along both — no single root, many entries,
each term feeding back on the others.

- **Agency (the inner axis):** *attention → imagination → possibility.* What you
  attend to sets what you can imagine; what you can imagine sets what you can hold
  as possible. This is why the enclosure of attention is *the enclosure underneath
  all the other enclosures* — capture the attention and you never need to suppress
  the conclusion it would reach (G-EP-013). "Attention is the root" is not idealism;
  it is the first link.
- **Action (the outer axis):** *goals → means → outcomes.* The means shape the
  outcome so completely that *the means are the ends, every time* (G-10-030).

Not two parallel lines but one rhizome: possibility feeds goals, outcomes retrain
attention, and any point can be entered first. The *paths* form enacts it — many
threads through one field, no privileged root. The two axes meet at the book's
own thesis: consciousness as terrain for revolution.

## The master principle

Every concept here is a tool that was turned against us and can be taken back.
The spell becomes the counter-spell. The averaged self becomes the gift. That
reversal is the wizard's whole art — and the reason a concept and its mirror
are ONE entry with two directions, not two entries.

**The asymmetry is primary.** Following Hardt and Negri, every form of the
commons has a corrupted form, and every magical technique has both. Some
schools come in motion-opposite pairs; some are modality alternatives; some
form a triad; some stand alone with the direction-pair internal. Magic does
not reduce to binaries — and the lexicon's shape teaches that.

## 1. The Terrain — *enclosure ⇄ commons*

The living world and the one axis everything moves along. To enclose is to
capture the way to a thing — stand in the doorway, charge at the gate. To
common is to hold it open and tend it together.

*Cognates:* enclosure, commons, the knowing between us, the pre-individual field.

---

## 2. Magic — *the average ⇄ the singular*

The unique transformative capacity of singularities acting on each other. In a
person it is **the gift** — your magic, the one vantage that is yours alone. In
the land it is **the secret ways** — the magic of place, the paths and cracks
no grid can capture, the route around the door. The averaging machine reduces
singularities to interchangeable units, and where there is no singularity there
is no magic.

*Cognates:* singularity, intersectionality, exodus, transindividuation, the gift.

### Types of magic — 9 schools

Each school holds both directions; the asymmetry between schools is part of the
system. The reader's job is to recognize *which* school is being cast.

#### Motion-opposite pairs (2 pairs, 4 schools)

**Conjuration ⇄ Abjuration** — *gather ⇄ exclude*

- **Conjuration:** call forth. Gather by demonstrating need (mutual aid, the
  strike, the march, the union) or by demonstrating confidence (the joint-stock
  corporation, the IPO). Summon a demon by naming it (the witch named for the
  burning; *terrorist* applied to make the dispossession legible).
- **Abjuration:** cast out. Racial and gender hierarchy. Redlining. The Lost
  Cause's enforced forgetting. The starved episteme. *Or* the community
  refusing the police, the Mbuti refusal of chiefs, Palmares' refusal to
  harbor slave-catchers — abjuration cast for the commons is the boundary
  that protects the commoning.

**Evocation ⇄ Transformation** — *felt mastery ⇄ technical mastery of elements*

- **Evocation:** the psychospiritual register. Music, art, ritual, ceremony.
  Brand identity and the national anthem (enclosure). The blues, the freedom
  song, the street mural, the block party, prayer-as-commons (commons).
- **Transformation:** the technical register. Science, technology, engineering.
  Patented monoculture, surveillance infrastructure, the algorithmic feed
  (enclosure). Open-source, the seed library, community solar, ham radio,
  Linux (commons).

#### Temporal pair (1 pair, 2 schools)

**Necromancy ⇄ Prefiguration** — *past-acting ⇄ future-acting*

- **Necromancy:** the dead acting on the living. *Commons cast:* Gramsci's
  organic intellectuals, Robinson's Black radical tradition — solidarity
  with those killed before you were born, the lineage carried forward.
  *Enclosure cast:* the Confederate flag as phylactery, the Lost Cause as
  ritual reanimation, "heritage," sovereign debt binding the unborn.
- **Prefiguration:** the future acting on the present. *Commons cast:* the
  Zapatista autonomy zone acting as if the new world is here, Cooperation
  Jackson operating cooperatively in a non-cooperative economy, AFSA building
  seed sovereignty inside a patent regime, build-and-defend-the-commons
  as practice now. *Enclosure cast:* the cargo cult (mimicking form without
  substance), the "innovation district" sign, astroturf, the performative
  future that holds the present in place.

#### Structure-magic triad (3 schools)

Three operations on the subject-structure relationship — not a binary because
the relation between subject and hidden/coded structure has three distinct
moves, not two.

- **Illusion** — *hide.* Manufacture ignorance. Misdirect attention. The
  color-blind market, the polished listing, *Nothing we can do*, the cleaned-
  up museum (enclosure). The Underground Railroad, maroon camouflage, the
  free school under the radar — illusion cast for commons is hiding-as-
  protection.
- **Divination** — *reveal.* The structure is real and hidden. See it.
  Robinson on racial capitalism, Rodney on underdevelopment, Federici on
  the witch hunts, Crenshaw on intersectionality, Du Bois on the wages of
  whiteness, Ostrom on working commons (commons). Surveillance capitalism,
  the credit score, the colonial cartographer, predictive policing
  (enclosure).
- **Deterritorialization** — *rupture.* Break the code so new becomings can
  emerge. *Commons cast:* the Black radical tradition rupturing the Marxist
  code, the Zapatista uprising rupturing Mexican sovereignty, the Combahee
  River Collective rupturing unitary feminism, migration rupturing borders,
  carnival, the maroon community walking out of the plantation. *Enclosure
  cast:* capital fleeing labor protections, gentrification rupturing place-
  bonds, fast fashion uprooting tradition, the algorithmic acceleration of
  attention. *Wizard's discipline:* deterritorialize *then reterritorialize*
  toward commons — re-code the freed space as commons, not as the next thing
  to enclose.

### Techniques of magic — how casting happens

Techniques interrelate. A sigil involves inscription, may invoke a pact, gets
activated through ritual. You usually need more than one at a time. A technique
is *how* a school is cast; the schools name the *kind* of work being done.

- **Incantation** — casting subjectivity through speech. Works on the mind.
  *Cast on enclosure:* the slow speech that turns *the way things are* into
  *the way things must be*. *Cast for commons:* the counter-spell that names
  a thing so you see it coming, and the room changes.
- **Invocation** — calling forth by name. Summons what is named; binds what
  is named. The witch invoked for the burning; the comrade invoked into being
  by the address; the demon you cannot face until you have its name. Naming
  is the first move of most casting.
- **Inscription** — marking. On the body (the gait, the gaze, the shrug
  taught early) AND on the terrain (the redline, the property line, the road
  that bypasses the village). Territorialization is inscription on land. The
  body's inscription compounds across generations.
- **Ritual** — repetition that conditions reality. *Nothing we can do*, said
  until it sounds like a fact. The meeting that happens every Tuesday and
  does nothing. The flag raised every morning. Cast for commons: the
  greeting that says *I see you* every time. The shared meal.
- **Sigil** — symbol or object that carries the spell forward. The flag,
  the brand, the deed, the receipt, the badge. The Confederate flag is a
  sigil functioning as phylactery (binding a dead spell to the living
  generation). The mutual-aid logo is a sigil too — the question is which
  direction it's cast.
- **Pact** — binding agreement across time. Contract, treaty, debt,
  constitution, the oath of office. Cast for enclosure: sovereign debt,
  the student loan, the non-compete. Cast for commons: the cooperative
  bylaws, the union contract, the consensus held over generations.

## 3. The Grimoire

A working list of what acts on you, drawn so you can act back. Each force it
names is a **demon** — not a devil but a power with a nature, a weakness, a use.
The book in your hands is one.

*Cognates:* theory/analysis as a tool for praxis; the demon.

---

## 4. The Wizard

One who has walked the terrain long enough to know it when it rises again, and
reaches to be useful: reads the field, wields the counter-spell, carries a gift,
knows the secret ways. Not a robe or a tower — a knowing held low, in the hands.
The capacities: name, read, build, hold.

Reserved for those whose explicit practice is to alter consciousness — Federici,
Fanon, Freire, Combahee. Sharp analysts who read the terrain rigorously without
practicing the counter-spell as their method (Luxemburg, Robinson, Rodney,
Mbembe) are not "wizards" in the strict sense, even when their work is
indispensable to the wizard's grimoire.

*Cognates:* the organic intellectual; the radicalized subject.

---

## 5. Initiation

The walk by which one becomes a wizard; it changes what you can bear to see,
with no graduation. You were initiated once already — into the spell. This is
the second. No station last; the wizard returns capable, never home.

*Cognates:* radicalization; praxis as ongoing; conscientization.
