# The Finish-the-Sentence pass — next-pass instruction

**Set by Jason, 2026-06-17**, merging two findings from the critique loop into one law:
the general pattern (below) and its largest instance, **metacausality**. This is the
through-line for the next pass on the whole river.

---

## The law (the kept instruction, verbatim)

> The one irony all four critics circled (Logic said it best): the book's signature move —
> **full-strength assertion over three-quarter-strength earning, the register covering the
> seam** — is now committed inside the one chapter whose subject is honest verification. The
> grace note: **in three of four faults, the chapter already contains its own correction and
> just doesn't finish the sentence.**

Read those two sentences together and they stop being a critique and become a *method*. The
fault is never a **missing** correction — it is an **unfinished** one. The book half-states
the truer, narrower, defensible claim somewhere nearby, and then overshoots it under cover of
the register. So the pass is:

- **NOT** "lower the mystical to the analytical" — that guts the book.
- **NOT** "invent a defense" — that violates the non-hallucination rule.
- **IS** *finish the sentence the book already started.* Find where the book already states
  the truer claim, and complete the move. The correction is **recombination, not invention** —
  the gold is already in the crack (kintsugi). The book corrects itself; the pass finishes its
  moves.

## The method (per overclaim)

1. **Find the overclaim** — the full-strength assertion the register is carrying past what was
   earned.
2. **Find the correction already on the page** — almost always within the same gesture or
   chapter: the narrower, truer claim the book states elsewhere and then overshoots.
3. **Finish the sentence** — *either* lower the overclaim to that claim, *or* **mark the
   boundary** so the claim is judged by the right standard. Never invent; locate and complete.
4. **Critic-check** in fresh context (the loop). Stage; Jason moves the river.

## Metacausality — the largest single instance of the law

The book's enchanted vocabulary — *magic, spell, demon, djinn, enchantment, incantation, the
natal chart, "by design," the wizard, the counter-spell* — is **metacausal, not causal.** It
names a real pattern by **correspondence**; it does not assert a **mechanism**. This is the
book's actual epistemology (Jung's **synchronicity** — the acausal connecting principle;
Hermetic **correspondence** — "as above, so below"); the magic *is* the metacausal layer.

So the whole "woo-as-argument" critique thread is a **register misread**: a critic (or
skeptic) reads a *metacausal* claim as a failed *causal* one — a misread the prose enables by
leaving the register unmarked. The standing meta-diagnosis was one level off: the mystical
strength is **metacausal strength**, a *different kind* of claim judged by a different standard
(*does the correspondence illuminate?*). The seam the register covers is exactly the
**causal/metacausal boundary** — and "finishing the sentence" here means **marking that
boundary.**

**The case that surfaced it.** "My natal Chiron is in Gemini, so conversation is difficult for
me" reads as causal (the placement *makes* it); read carefully it is metacausal (placement and
trait are the same pattern in two languages — the "so" is legibility, not mechanism). **Kept**,
because on its own terms it is true.

### Keep / Mark / Fix
1. **KEEP** metacausal claims at full mystical strength. Do not apologize, hedge, or convert
   them to analytic prose. They are the book's epistemology.
2. **MARK** the boundary wherever a metacausal claim is liable to be misread as causal — one
   light touch, *correspondence not mechanism* — so the skeptic judges it by the right standard.
3. **FIX** the genuine slides, where a *causal* overclaim hides under mystical cover. Lower it
   to the metacausal claim already supported — usually truer and stronger.

## What this pass is NOT
- **Not de-enchantment.** Keep full mystical strength; only the causal slide is lowered.
- **Not invention.** Only completion of moves the book already makes; provenance or it doesn't
  ship.
- Chapter-level river order stays sacred; same staging + fresh-context critic-loop contract.
