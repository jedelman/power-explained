# The metacausal pass — register principle & instruction

**Principle stated by Jason, 2026-06-17**, from the natal-Chiron reading in the critique
loop. This is the through-line for the next pass on the whole river.

---

## The principle

The book's enchanted vocabulary — *magic, spell, demon, djinn, enchantment, incantation, the
natal chart, "by design," the wizard, the counter-spell* — is **metacausal, not causal.** It
names and illuminates a real pattern by **correspondence**. It does not assert a supernatural
or hidden **mechanism**.

**The case that surfaced it.** "My natal Chiron is in Gemini, so conversation is difficult for
me" reads, to a skeptic, as a *causal* claim — the placement *makes* the difficulty. Read
carefully it is *metacausal*: the placement and the trait are the **same pattern read in two
languages** — sky and self — not cause and effect. The "so" is a *so* of legibility and
correspondence, not of mechanism. Kept, because on its own terms it is true. (The critics —
and skeptical readers — misread it as causal *because the prose did not mark the register*.)

This is the book's **actual epistemology** — the one its alchemy / Goetia / astrology idiom
already runs on: Jung's **synchronicity** (the *acausal connecting principle*), Hermetic
**correspondence** ("as above, so below"). The magic *is* the metacausal layer. "The angle on
the world that is yours alone" is a metacausal fact; the averaging machine destroys the
metacausal (the singular) and keeps only the causal (the average). To strip the enchantment
out for analytic safety would be to side with the averaging machine.

## Why it matters — it dissolves the whole "woo-as-argument" critique thread

Every time a critic (or a skeptic) flags *"the magic is doing the work an argument should,"*
they are reading a **metacausal claim as a failed causal one** — a misread the prose *enables*
by leaving the register unmarked. The standing meta-diagnosis ("full mystical strength
asserted where three-quarter analytical strength was earned; the register covers the seam")
was one level off: the mystical strength is **metacausal strength** — a *different kind* of
claim, judged by a different standard (*does the correspondence illuminate?*), not a weaker
version of a causal claim. The seam the register covers is exactly the **causal / metacausal
boundary.**

So the fix is **not** "lower the mystical to the analytical" — that guts the book. It is
**"mark the register, so each claim is judged by the right standard."** Full mystical strength
is appropriate to metacausal claims; analytic rigor is appropriate to causal ones. The only
faults live at the **unmarked slides.**

## The test (run every enchanted / symbolic claim through it)

Ask: **is this doing metacausal work or causal work?**

- **Metacausal** — naming, illuminating, corresponding to a real pattern. ("Enclosure is a
  spell." "The corruption-of-the-commons demon." "Magic is when particular things become one."
  Chiron the wounded healer.) → **KEEP**, at full strength. This is the gold.
- **Causal-masquerade** — a claim that reads as, or smuggles in, an efficient-causal
  *mechanism* under mystical cover; **or** a metacausal claim left so unmarked it reads as a
  failed causal one. → **MARK** or **FIX**.

## The rule

1. **KEEP** metacausal claims at full strength. Do not apologize, hedge, or convert them to
   analytic prose. They are the book's epistemology.
2. **MARK** the boundary wherever a metacausal claim is liable to be misread as causal — one
   light touch that signals *correspondence, not mechanism* — so the skeptic judges it by the
   right standard instead of bouncing.
3. **FIX** the genuine slides, where a *causal* overclaim hides under mystical cover. These are
   the real overclaims the critics keep finding. Lower them to the metacausal claim actually
   supported — which is usually truer and stronger.

## Worked examples (from the 06-17 critique)

- **KEEP (metacausal, already legible):** natal Chiron in Gemini; the wounded-healer image;
  "magic is what happens when particular things meet"; the demons of *The Speaking of Names*.
- **FIX (causal slide) — done this session:**
  - **The wager** — "love performed deploys real force" reads as efficient causation.
    Metacausally it *equips the caster* (final causation toward the reader's agency); the force
    is the reader's, downstream. → rescoped.
  - **The keystone** — "find that it **was**" asserts a pre-existing causal fact. Metacausally /
    emergently it *holds* — proven in the building, not prior to it. → fixed.
  - **The loom / djinn** — the loom is the *causal* production (neutral); the djinn is the
    *metacausal* figure of the relation (the danger that appears in the presentation). Two
    names, two levels. → clarified.
- **FIX — queued for the pass (the big one):**
  - **Functionalism** — "it is policy / serves someone" reads as a *designer-cause*
    (conspiracy-shaped). Metacausally it names a **self-maintaining function** — "no plan, only
    maintenance," which the book already says (l.56/716). The unmarked causal/metacausal
    boundary here *is* the falsifier every critic has asked for: mark that "by design" means
    *functions as if designed*, not *designed by someone*, and the morning's #1 hole closes
    without weakening the claim.
